2.5.3 Description Lists (Financial Glossary)

Liquidity
The ease with which an asset can be converted into cash without affecting its market price.
Leverage
The use of borrowed capital to finance an investment.
Fiduciary
A person or organization that acts on behalf of another person or persons to manage assets.
