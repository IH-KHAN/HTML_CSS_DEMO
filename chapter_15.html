15.2 Hex Colors (Corporate Branding)

Hexadecimal colors (e.g., `#RRGGBB`) provide the exact precision needed for corporate identity. SecureBank uses specific Hex codes for its logo and UI elements.

Brand Blue

Usage: Hex codes are the industry standard for web design due to their compactness and widespread support.

15.3 RGB Colors (Dynamic Data)

RGB values (`rgb(red, green, blue)`) define colors by intensity (0-255). RGBA adds an **Alpha channel** (0-1) for transparency, useful for overlays and financial charts.
