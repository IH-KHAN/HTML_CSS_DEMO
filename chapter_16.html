16.3 Text Position (Alignment)

16.3.1 Text Align

Centered Title

Balance: $10,500.00 (Right-aligned for currency)

Justified Text: This paragraph is justified. In formal banking documents, justified text creates clean edges on both the left and right sides, similar to a printed newspaper column.


16.3.2 Text Indent

This paragraph has a first-line indentation. This style is often used in formal letters or contract clauses to visually separate paragraphs without adding extra margin space.
